Go-to-Market Strategy, Partnerships & Strategic Growth

I work one-on-one with a small number of clients each year — as a trusted right hand during periods of scale or transition.

The work is specific: go-to-market strategy for new businesses and brands, partnership strategies that create leverage without trading more of your time, and strategic growth decisions that shape what the business becomes next.

Most engagements begin at an inflection point — a launch, a rebrand, a new revenue line, a pivot, a season when the person building needs someone in the room who’s been there before and will tell them the truth.

Who This Is For

Who This Is For

You’re building something.

A new business, a new brand, a new product line, a new chapter inside an existing company. You might be a founder, an entrepreneur, a creator who’s evolved into an operator, or someone leading growth inside a business that’s scaling fast. The label matters less than the moment you’re in.

You’ve proven something real. Now you’re at an inflection point — a launch, a pivot, a period of scale — and you need a strategic partner who can see the whole picture. Someone who will tell you the truth, help you make the right calls, and stay in the room long enough for the work to compound.

You’ve worked across industries long enough to know that the best strategists aren’t tethered to one vertical. You want someone who’s built and launched inside consumer brands, creator businesses, hospitality, financial services, technology, and media — and who brings that pattern recognition to your business.

Who This Isn’t For

Who This Isn’t For

People still in the idea phase. Anyone looking for quick hacks, tactical consulting, or done-for-you agency work.

I don’t take on development-heavy builds, I don’t run campaigns, and I don’t write playbooks. I’m strategy, partnerships, and direction — the thinking partner you keep close during the seasons that matter.

How I Work

How I Work

Three lenses inform every engagement:

Brand Positioning & Partnerships. Becoming the obvious choice in your space. Messaging that converts the right people and repels the wrong ones. Partnership strategies that generate revenue without trading more of your time.

Digital Product & Technology Strategy. Building scalable revenue streams — digital products, automated systems — so you’re not the bottleneck in your own business.

Operations & Internal Systems. Streamlining the backend so you get your time, attention, and decision-making capacity back.

All of it filtered through an energy-first lens. The best strategic decisions start with getting honest about why you’re building, what you actually value, and the season you’re in. Strategy without that foundation is just more noise.

About

About

I’ve spent nearly two decades building brands, businesses, and products — starting in digital media at The Weather Channel, then joining an early-stage women’s lifestyle media company (PureWow) as one of its earliest employees, where I grew into VP of Product leading design, engineering, and product teams. Following the 2018 acquisition by Gary Vaynerchuk and the launch of Gallery Media Group, I led the visual and product experience across a growing portfolio of digital media brands.

In 2019 I founded The Day Consulting, which evolved from a boutique agency into highly selective advisory work. Over seven years I’ve advised 50+ founders across creators, hospitality, financial services, consumer brands, and technology startups.

In parallel, I lead growth and operations for my family’s commercial services business, where we’re building a services holding company focused on acquiring and scaling service-based businesses.

I write The Business of Energy, a Substack on using energy as a primary navigation system for life and work. I’m a mother of three.
